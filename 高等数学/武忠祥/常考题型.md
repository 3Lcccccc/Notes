1. $\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} a_n=a\rightarrow\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} |a_n|=|a|$

1. $\sin x<x<tan x，x\in(0,\frac{\pi}{2})$
2. $\frac{x}{1+x}<\ln(1+x)<x，x\in(0,+\infty)$
